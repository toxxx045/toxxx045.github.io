Auto-Dose is a concept design for an auto-injection device. It's structure is based off that of micro pipetters found in biological labs. The precision and ease of isolating small volumes makes pipette technology ideal for creating an adjustable, personalized auto-injection device. Because medication doses and needle depth requirements vary among individual patients, simplicity in adjusting these parameters are necessary. Moreover, auto-injection syringes are expensive and often only one-time use. Therefore, this resuable auto-injection housing is more cost effective, allowing cheaper syringes to be used as refills.
